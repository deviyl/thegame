The Game is simple. Wager as many xanax as you wish and click Play. The Game will decide if you win or lose. Winning doubles your wagered amount. Lose and your wager is lost to The Game.

HOW TO WITHDRAW
To withdraw your balance, send a message to Deviyl [3722358] with the topic set to The Game Payout. Your full balance will be paid out within 24 hours.

Attention: The topic must be set properly for payout. The message content is irrelevant. If the topic is not set correctly, The Game will not acknowledge your payout request.
